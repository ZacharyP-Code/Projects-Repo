Finance Concepts Using the WoW Economy

Supply and Demand

In the Auction House, when many players farm the same item, supply increases and prices drop. When items are rare or needed for raids, demand increases and prices rise.

Investing Strategy

Players can purchase undervalued materials and resell them later at higher prices. This mirrors real-world investing strategies based on buying low and selling high.

Risk and Reward

Not every investment works. Market prices can change based on patches, updates, or player behavior. Higher potential profits often come with higher risk.
